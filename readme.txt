Made in unity 2020.2.4f1. Tested to work on new Android phone


To use AR + VR:
Open helloAR scene
This mode is in VR with position tracking capabilities. When you move in real life, you will also move a distance in the game. 
Note: Requires high phone specs or it might not run. Multiple light source in the environment may cause bugs to happen. 


To use VR:
Open vrmode scene 


How to use:
Select build for android, put your phone into the google cardboard and turn on the app.
